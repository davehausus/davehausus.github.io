TOWN GREEN

Designing a tool to inform the community

OVERVIEW

The Town Green at Scotts Valley, California is a US$180 million mixed-use development that will feature housing, retail, restaurants, public art and green space. The entity formed to develop this project was in need of a website with the main goal to inform the Scotts Valley Community what is getting approved.

GOAL

To have the website live in a period of 2 weeks (before the first event with the community). Need to be mobile first, and due to the lack of information available at the moment, must be created in a way that it can eventually be the home page of a larger site that is created over the next 6 months.

ROLE

The website will be a key tool to communicate to the community so it is important to have the residents to submit their contact details and input into a database so we can send them information in the future as it becomes available.

CASE TIMELINE

2018 - 2019 September

WIREFRAMING

Solution for the morning preparation with every guy’s first date in mind by creating a neat branding finding a self lenguage with the minimal elements possible. Taking care about everything goes right at that special day is the main goal and best briefing possible.
