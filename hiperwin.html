HIPERWIN

Brand identity for a Home Appliance company

OVERVIEW

Hiperwin imports and distributes Home Appliances for sale online. The company was about to import thousands of products and was in need of a brand and an identity that at the time was non-existent.

GOAL

Design the brand and identity within 6 weeks, so that packaging, manuals and products can be printed with the supplier in China and shipped to Mexico in time for a huge online sale.

ROLE

I was in charge of the brand design, identity, copywriting and refreshing the online store according to the new guidelines.

CASE TIMELINE

July 2020 - August 2020

The Brand

The requirement was to design the brand identifier, as well as one identifier for each of its product lines. The brand symbol aims to emulate the top view of various home appliances. The meaning: The whole is greater than the sum of the parts.

The shapes in the symbol have 3 sizes, and increments in the shapes are based on the Fibonacci sequence.

Each product line has been assigned a unique geometric shape and color, the brand is a composition that mixes these shapes and colors. To prevent confusion or brand dilution, each time a line identifier is used, it must be accompanied by the legend "by Hiperwin".

Printed Applications

The primary use of these appliances is to cut / slice food, the design incorporates these characteristics through prominent angles and stripes.

By the time the packaging was designed, the products were not available for photography, so the labeling of the products that appear on the boxes had to be done digitally and should look realistic.

CONVERSATIONAL DESIGN

These boxes are designed in a way that it is almost certain they will be opened from the top. So when customers open the box the first thing they will see will be a thank you message.

Web Applications

The online store initially had a default layout, it was updated to incorporate these new concepts.

The update of the store was not only visually, the main menu and the content structure were modified to increase the visibility for the new products.
