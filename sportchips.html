SPORTCHIPS MEXICO

Taking Chip Tuning to a new era

OVERVIEW

Sportchips is a company dedicated to the automotive industry, they develop platforms for vehicles to generate adequate benefits to the environment of a specific country. Its main product is a configuration specially developed so that any vehicle can recover and perfect the functions that influence its power source.

GOAL

Redesign their non-responsive website into a responsive one and include a new function that will allow users to find the application for a specific vehicle.

ROLE

I was in charge of redesigning, developing and updating the website, as well as managing the customer database. A team of developers was hired to make the Vehicle Finder Module work.

CASE TIMELINE

April 2015 - June 2016

THE PREVIOUS VERSION

By the end of 2008 I was hired as the only in-house web designer to design the first website of the company. At that time responsiveness was not a requirement.

The Redesign

THE HOME PAGE

The vast majority of website visitors already know the product and only want to know if the application exists for their vehicle. For this reason, the hero section, rather than a description of the product or the company, presents the stellar applications.

THE RESULTS PAGE

Once the user finds their vehicle, a list of available products is displayed in descending order of priority, each row should include some features as well as an image of the product.

THE PRODUCT PAGE

After selecting the desired product, a page with the complete information of the product is displayed, as well as a form to request a quote for it.
