ATHLYTIX

Modernizing Sports Analytics

OVERVIEW

Athlyti(x) brings advanced statistics to the everyday athlete. The app allows an individual to create a team(s) and record the statistics of a live basketball game, including everything from rebounds, turnovers, made/missed shots and even where the shot was taken on the court.

GOAL

Design an iPad app that is very user friendly, clean and simple for the amateur athlete and coach. The simpler and more intuitive, the better.

ROLE

I was in charge of designing the product and the Brand identity.

CASE TIMELINE

March 2014 - April 2014

WHO ELSE IS THERE?

HoopMetrics (white) and TurboStats (black) are the two main competitors. Although both are at opposite ends in the complexity spectrum, they have one thing in common, their dull UI make findability harder for users as it is not clear where they need to go to look for specific content, increasing dramatically the learning curve.

While capturing stats in a fast-paced sport such as basketball, relying heavily on memory will reduce the ability for users to complete tasks quickly.
